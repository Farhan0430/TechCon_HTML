History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small local event aimed at connecting tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a global conference attracting thousands of professionals from around the world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, support collaboration, and drive impactful change in the technology sector by bringing together diverse voices and perspectives.

Notable Past Speakers

We've hosted an array of inspiring speakers, including:

  • Dr. Jane Smith – AI researcher and advocate for ethical technology.
  • Carlos Diaz – Entrepreneur and founder of several successful tech startups.
  • Amina Yusuf – Cybersecurity expert and global keynote speaker.
